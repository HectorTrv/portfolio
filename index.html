About

Product Designer with 6+ years of experience working with startups, scale-ups and large corporations to build ambitious digital products — beautiful, functional and built to last.

I have a strong focus on complex problem spaces: large-scale design systems, AI-augmented design workflows, and close R&D collaboration with engineering teams. What drives me is designing products that hold up both strategically and experientially.

Outside of work, you'll find me boxing, sewing, exploring design in all its forms — graphic, industrial, fashion — or diving into the latest emerging technologies. Disciplines that all share the same obsession: craft, precision and intention.

Feel free to reach out if you have a project you'd like us to work on together.
